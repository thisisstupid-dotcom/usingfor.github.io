01 / The capstone

A platform with a chicken-and-egg problem

For my capstone, my team designed the Strategic Philanthropy Marketing Hub — a three-sided platform connecting Hillview Estate, The King's Foundation Australia, and the donors and businesses around them, built around UN Sustainable Development Goal 17, Partnerships for the Goals.

The hard part

A three-sided platform is worth nothing until all three sides show up — and none will show up for an empty platform. This is the classic cold-start problem, and solving it was the real marketing challenge.

My role

  • Team framing. I led the introduction and conclusion that tied eight separate chapters into one coherent argument.
  • The launch question. I owned the question every marketer eventually faces — this is a great idea, but how do you actually launch it? That's the next page.
